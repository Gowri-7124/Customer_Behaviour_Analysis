# Customer Behavior Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer Behavior Analysis is a data analytics project focused on understanding customer purchasing patterns, shopping trends, and business insights from retail data. The project uses data analysis, database management, and visualization techniques to transform raw customer data into meaningful business intelligence.

The main objective of this project is to analyze customer behavior and identify patterns related to purchasing habits, revenue generation, customer loyalty, product popularity, and subscription trends.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze customer purchasing behavior
* Identify high-value customers and loyal buyers
* Study product performance and customer ratings
* Understand the impact of discounts and subscriptions
* Compare spending patterns across different customer groups
* Generate business insights through dashboards and reports
